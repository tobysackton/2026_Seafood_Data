The connection: The temporal premium tracked the import rush. As cumulative imports flattened—indicating buyers had secured their inventory—the urgency-driven premium dissipated. By October, with imports approaching their asymptote, the differential had converged to nearly the structural baseline.
Methodology: Structural component derived from regression-predicted differential at 2025's Gulf supply share (22.1%). Temporal component = observed differential minus structural prediction. Import data from U.S. customs statistics; cumulative totals combine Newfoundland and New Brunswick origins. Pack Year 2025 runs April 2025 through March 2026.
